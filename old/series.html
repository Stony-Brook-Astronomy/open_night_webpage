Our colleagues in Physics, the Geosciences and Ecology and Evolution have parallel programs of lectures. Their schedules are found at:

  • The Worlds of Physics
    Generally the second Friday of the month. Our physics colleagues tell us about some exciting developments in physics.
     
  • The Living World
    Generally the third Friday of the month. These lectures are given by the ecology and evolution department.
     
  • Geology Open Night
    Generally the fourth Friday of the month. These lectures are given by our geology colleagues (They serve coffee!).
     
    Also see the L.I. Geologists programs.
These lectures are also only given during the school year and are held at 7:30pm in ESS 001.
